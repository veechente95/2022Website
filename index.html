About Me

My name is Vicente Ceja (he/him). I'm a self-taught python developer from Boyle Heights, California seeking to launch a career in the world of tech. My background consists of working with Geographic Information Systems and Technology (GIS&T) and completed my undergraduate studies at UCLA in 2018. On my freetime I love to record music, workout, bake and cook.

My Experience and Goal

My Experience

For as long as I can remember, I have always been fascinated by the world of tech. Coming from a career that focuses on GIS mapping, data management and non-profit organizational work, I genuinely believed I was never good enough; However, I learned to accept the vulnerability and excitement to start again, stretch my limts and teach myself how to code.

My Goal

As a first generation Mexican-American, I acknowledge the lack of diversity in traditional STEM fields and want to break the stigma by providing a diverse open door for future POC and Latinx folks coming from different communities, backgrounds and technical abilities. I want to use my story to help guide others who may have also experienced similar cases of imposter syndrome and help inspire them.
